The Leviathan returned, now corrupted by Nightmares from humanity's past. With its sights set on the Moon's dormant Pyramid ship, Guardians intervened before Calus could claim its power.
In the end, the Leviathan was severed from the Pyramid permanently. But even as the Nightmares began to fade, Calus ensured his return and threatened the Vanguard with the arrival of the Witness.
